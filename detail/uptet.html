Information Uttar Pradesh Basic Education Board, Government of Uttar Pradesh has announced notification for conducting Uttar Pradesh Teacher Eligibility Test (UPTET) 2021 to select Teachers for Classes I-V (Primary Level), Classes VI-VIII (Upper Primary Level).
Application Fees For Single Paper I or II:
For General/ OBC: Rs. 600/-
For SC/ ST: Rs. 400/-
For PwD: Rs.100/-
For Both Papers:
For General/ OBC: Rs. 1200/-
For SC/ ST: Rs. 800/-
For PwD: Rs.200/-
Important Dates Starting Date for Apply: 18-05-2021
Last Date for Submit: 01-06-2021
Last Date to Submit Fee: 02-06-2021
Last Date to Submit Completed Registration/ Print Application Form: 03-06-2021
Date for Uploading of Admit Card on Website: 14-07-2021
Date for Exam (Paper I & II): 25-07-2021
Date for Uploading of Answer Key on Website: 02-08-2021
Date for Declaration of Final Key & Result: 20-08-2021
Qualification Primary Level:
Passed 10+2 (Intermediate) Exam with 50% Marks and 4 Year B.LEd Degree OR Bachelor Degree in Any Stream with B.Ed Degree OR Diploma in Teacher from Aligarh Muslim University AMU, Aligarh OR Moaalizm E Urdu Exam Passed OR D.Ed Special Exam Passed 2 Year BTC Or Special BTC Exam Passed
Junior Level:
Bachelor Degree/ Master Degree with 50% Marks with B.Ed / B.Ed Special Exam Passed OR Bachelor Degree/ Master Degree with BTC Training 2 Year Exam Passed OR 10+2 Intermediate Exam with 50% Marks and B.LEd 4 Year Course OR 10+2 Inter Exam with 50% Marks and B.SC Ed Exam Passed.
